Introduction

The IN-MOBILITY project was created with the goal of transforming underutilized urban spaces into vibrant and functional places, promoting sustainable mobility. The heart of the project lies in the union between the school community — made up of students and teachers — and the regeneration of urban spaces. Our approach is simple yet ambitious: to move immobile places towards a more sustainable and accessible future for all. IN-MOBILITY is a project that aims to make a difference by directly involving the school and the local community in a transformation process focused on the future, sustainability, and collective well-being. A small step for great mobility. This is a project of urban regeneration and sustainable mobility, led by Capofila Urbana Cooperativa Sociale.

The IN-MOBILITÀ project aims to regenerate unused urban spaces, making them lively and functional, with a particular focus on sustainable mobility. At the heart of the initiative is the collaboration between schools and the local community to transform the area in a participatory and future-oriented way. The goal is to promote a concrete shift towards a more accessible and sustainable urban environment.

Project Goals

Regeneration of spaces for sustainable mobility

Our primary goal is to regenerate an area dedicated to sustainable mobility by creating spaces that encourage the use of eco-friendly means of transport. We're talking about bike-sharing stations and parking areas for electric vehicles.

Spazi scolastici innovativi e green

Within this project, another key objective is the development of spaces aligned with the interests of schools, such as a wooden classroom surrounded by greenery.

Data collection on home-to-school travel.

Understanding students’ travel patterns is essential for improving sustainable mobility, through surveys and analysis of travel habits.

Webapp

PlaNet is a sustainable education platform designed to be accessible, engaging, and stimulating. It focuses on key topics such as sustainable food, circular economy, sustainable mobility, energy consumption, and diversity and inclusion (D&I). By using gamification as a learning technique, PlaNet makes skill acquisition an interactive and motivating experience, fostering greater awareness and responsibility toward a more sustainable and inclusive future.
